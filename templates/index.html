Welcome to HAND_2_WRITE

Our platform provides essential support for disabled individuals, particularly those with anophthalmia (without eyes) and amputations (missing limbs/hands), by connecting them with qualified scribes for their examinations.

In today's world, disabled individuals often face significant challenges in finding reliable scribes/writers for their exams. Our innovative online platform bridges this gap by creating a direct connection between those in need and available writers.

Through our comprehensive system, disabled persons can register their requirements with assistance, while we maintain an extensive database of verified writers. Our matching algorithm then pairs candidates with the most suitable scribe based on availability, location, and specific needs.
