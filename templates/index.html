Accessible Exam Support

WELCOME TO HAND_2_WRITE

A Help for Disabled Person (specially for Anophthalmia - Person without eyes & Amputee - person missing two limbs/Hands) to find their scribe to write their exams.

Nowadays, disabled individuals, especially those with anophthalmia or amputations, face difficulty in finding a scribe/writer for their exams. Our website aims to help them by providing an online platform to connect with scribes/writers.

We have created a website where a disabled person can enter their details with the assistance of another person. On the other hand, we also maintain a database of available writers.

Based on availability, our website will match and provide them with a suitable scribe/writer.
